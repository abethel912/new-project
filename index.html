Top Five Places To See Sunset on Cape Cod

  1. Grey's Beach, Yarmouth

    2. Located in Yarmouthport, Gray's Beach provides a magnificient sunset with a long boardwalk leading up to it. In addition, there is plenty of wildlife that can be found in the marshes on both sides of the boardwalk.

  2. Mayflower Beach, Dennis

    3. The sunset at Mayflower is at it's best during low tide. With dunes that stretch for miles, this beach does not disappoint.

  3. Paine's Creek Beach, Brewster
  4. Rock Harbor, Orleans
  5. Race Point Beach, Provincetown
